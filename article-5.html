Best Robot Vacuums 2025: Roborock vs Roomba vs Eufy

Robot vacuums have evolved from novelty gadgets to essential home appliances. Modern models can map your entire home, avoid obstacles, and even empty themselves. We've tested the latest generation to find which ones are actually worth the investment.

1. Best Overall: Roborock S8 Pro Ultra

The Roborock S8 Pro Ultra is the most advanced robot vacuum we've tested. It features a self-emptying dock that also washes and dries the mop pads, refills the water tank, and has 6000Pa suction. The sonic mopping vibrates 3000 times per minute to lift stuck-on stains.

2. Best Value: Eufy X10 Pro Omni

The Eufy X10 Pro Omni offers premium features at a mid-range price. It includes auto-empty, auto-mop-washing, and impressive 8000Pa suction. For under $800, it's the best bang for your buck in the robot vacuum market.

Check Price on Amazon

3. Best for Pet Owners: iRobot Roomba j7+

The iRobot Roomba j7+ is specifically designed for homes with pets. It uses AI to avoid pet waste (yes, poop detection is real), and the dual rubber brushes prevent hair tangles. iRobot will even replace your vacuum for free if it fails to avoid pet accidents.
